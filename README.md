**Task Title**

Understanding the fundamentals of Cyber Security and Attack Surface Analysis

**Description**

This task involves studying the core concepts of cyber security with an emphasis
on understanding how digital systems are protected against threats. It covers
the CIA Triad (Confidentiality, Integrity, and Availability), different types of
cyber attackers, commonly exposed attack surfaces, OWASP Top 10 vulnerabilities,
the flow of data within applications, and possible points where attacks may occur
in real-world scenarios.

**Tools Used**

Google web browser,Firefox,Chrome.

**Outcome**

Gained in-depth knowledge of foundational cyber security concepts, improved
awareness of common vulnerabilities, and developed the ability to identify
potential attack surfaces in everyday applications.
